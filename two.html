HTML entities :- Entities are strings of text that start with an ampersand(&) and end with a semicolon (;),These entities are commonly used to demonstrate characters that are reserved and imperceptible characters such as non breaking spaces.

5 Commanly used Html entities

  • less than sign : <
  • Greater than sign : >
  • Non-breaking space:  
  • the ampersand (and) sign : &
  • the double quote sign : "
